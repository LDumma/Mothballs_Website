Our Story

Mothballs Vintage Company was born out of a deep-rooted love for all things vintage.

High end retro fashion, charming pieces from days gone by are sought to provide to a larger audience. Today, we take pride in offering a selection of vintage goods that are as beautiful as they are unique.

Uncovering The Past For The Future

As lovers of history and aesthetics, we believe that vintage items hold a value beyond their price tag.

  • They represent a moment in time, a snapshot of history, and a testament to craftsmanship.
  • Every vintage dress, skirt, and retro jacket in our collection has a story to tell.
  • We see ourselves as the bridge, connecting these stories from the past to new generations who value sustainability, uniqueness, and authenticity.
